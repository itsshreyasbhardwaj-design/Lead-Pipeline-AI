# Discovery Questions Bank

A reference bank of discovery questions organized by category, used as raw material for `prompts/meeting-prep.md`. Select and adapt the ones relevant to your specific prospect rather than running through the whole list mechanically.

```markdown
## Situation Questions (understand current state)
- Walk me through how [relevant process] works for your team today.
- What are you currently using to handle [problem area]?
- How long has [current situation] been in place?

## Problem/Pain Questions (surface the cost of the status quo)
- What's the biggest bottleneck in [relevant process] right now?
- What happens when [specific failure mode] occurs — who does it affect?
- How much time/money would you estimate this costs you per [week/month]?

## Implication Questions (make the cost of inaction concrete)
- If this isn't solved in the next [timeframe], what happens?
- How does this affect [adjacent team/metric/goal]?
- Has this ever caused a bigger downstream problem? What happened?

## Priority/Timing Questions (gauge real urgency)
- Where does solving this rank against your other priorities this quarter?
- What would need to be true for this to become urgent?
- Is there a deadline or event driving this timeline?

## Buying Process Questions (map authority and process)
- Who else would be involved in evaluating a solution like this?
- What does your evaluation/approval process typically look like?
- Have you evaluated anything in this space before? What happened?

## Vision Questions (co-create the desired future state)
- If this were solved perfectly, what would that look like day-to-day?
- What would you need to see to feel confident this is working?
- How would you measure success six months in?
```
