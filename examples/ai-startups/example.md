# Example: AI Startup

All names, companies, and statistics below are fictional composites for demonstration purposes.

## Company Context

```markdown
## Company Overview
Company Name: Ledgerline AI
One-line description: AI agent that automates invoice reconciliation and
AP workflows for mid-market finance teams.
Founded / Stage: Seed, 14 employees, 8 months post-launch

## What We Sell
Product: Ledgerline AP Agent
Pricing model: Per-seat + usage tier based on invoice volume
Typical deal size: $15K–$60K ACV

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
Industry: Any mid-market company with a dedicated AP function
Company size: 200–2,000 employees
Geography: US
Stage: Post-Series B or private equity-backed (budget stability matters)
Technographic signals: NetSuite, QuickBooks Enterprise, or Sage Intacct in use
Buyer roles who initiate: Controller, AP Manager
Buyer roles who approve budget: CFO, VP Finance

## What We Solve
1. Manual invoice matching consumes 15-20 hours/week for a typical AP team
2. Reconciliation errors cause vendor payment delays and strained relationships
3. Finance teams can't scale AP headcount at the same rate as transaction volume
Cost of inaction: Continued manual labor cost, error-driven vendor friction,
inability to close books quickly
Why alternatives fall short: Legacy RPA tools are brittle and break on format
changes; generic AI tools lack finance-specific reconciliation logic

## Unique Advantages
Purpose-built reconciliation logic (not a generic LLM wrapper)
Proof point: Cut AP processing time 65% for a 400-person logistics company
in first 60 days
Competitors: Manual process (most common "competitor"), legacy RPA (Kofax, UiPath)

## Brand Voice
3 words we are: Technical, credible, finance-literate
3 words we are not: Hype-driven, vague-about-mechanism, "AI-magic" salesy
Tone: Speaks fluently about accounting workflows, not just AI capability

## Buying Triggers
1. Recent Series B+ funding (signals finance team scaling pressure)
2. Job posting for "AP Specialist" or "Staff Accountant" (signals current pain)
3. ERP migration announcement (NetSuite implementation, etc.)

## Success Metrics
Technical evaluation calls booked, pilot conversions

## Objections We Hear Most Often
1. "Is this just another AI wrapper?" — reframe: explain the purpose-built
   reconciliation logic and offer to show real match-rate benchmarks
2. "Worried about accuracy/trust for financial data" — reframe: human-in-the-loop
   approval workflow by default, full audit trail
3. "Too early stage / risk of vendor going away" — reframe: transparent about
   stage, offer month-to-month pilot terms to de-risk

## Social Proof
Customers: One 400-person logistics company (pilot), one 250-person healthcare
services company (fictional, named with permission in real usage)
Quantified results: 65% AP processing time reduction in 60 days
```
