# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in Lead Pipeline AI a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Submitting prompts or examples designed to deceive, manipulate, or scrape personal data without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope of Sales-Specific Conduct

Because this project produces outbound sales content, contributors must not submit prompts, examples, or workflows that:

- Encourage deceptive personalization (fabricating facts about a prospect)
- Encourage spam, unsolicited mass messaging without an opt-out path, or violation of CAN-SPAM/GDPR/CCPA
- Scrape or harvest personal data in a way that violates a platform's terms of service

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers by opening a confidential issue or contacting the repository owner directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
